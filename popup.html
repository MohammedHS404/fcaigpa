GPA Calculator

Follow these steps to calculate your GPA:

  1. Install the GPA Calculator extension in your Chrome browser.
  2. Navigate to Registered Courses:
    • http://193.227.14.58/#/courses-per-students
    • http://newecom.fci.cu.edu.eg/#/courses-per-students
  3. Click on the GPA Calculator icon in the Chrome toolbar.
  4. Click the Calculate GPA button below to extract data and compute your GPA.
